I'm a Pure.css grid

Just create a div with the class "pure-g" and next divs with classes of "pure-u-1 pure-u-md-*-*". Create a child div within your grid and add the class "grid-content" to it for customization. The grid_colors mixin will give you consistent colors throughout your design.
